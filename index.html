Submission Guidelines

  • Please shoot horizontally (for both video and photos!)
  • Find a quiet location without background noise and speak as clearly as you can
  • Please introduce yourself and your department at the beginning of your video
  • Try to limit your message to 30 seconds
  • If sending a photo, make sure to hold up a sign featuring your shoutout’s name and a message!
  • Send submissions to uvafmux@virginia.edu or 434-987-4332

About the virtual family greeting card series

This is a fun, positive way to bring us together and feel connected. Even as we’re all facing new circumstances, we’re still part of one big FM family.

Your submission is your chance to let someone know you’re thinking about them and will be combined with others’ messages in a series of short greeting card videos that will be shared within the FM family.

Each greeting card video will feature 5-8 shoutouts, so if you don’t see your shoutout right away, don’t worry! We will make as many video greeting cards as we have submissions for.

Questions + Troubleshooting

Please feel free to reach out to the FM User Experience & Engagement team with questions or for help troubleshooting any problems you run into.

You can reach us via email at uvafmux@virginia.edu.
